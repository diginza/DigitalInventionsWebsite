ABOUT US

Digital Inventions at a glance

At Digital Inventions, we believe that the right data, used the right way, can transform how non-profit organisations learn, grow, and deliver impact.

Founded by David Jacobs, who serves as Lead Consultant, Digital Inventions offers deep expertise grounded in two decades of experience in ICT, monitoring and evaluation (M&E), and non-profit leadership. Every project is personally led by David, ensuring a hands-on, thoughtful approach to solving complex data challenges.

Digital Inventions works flexibly, drawing on a trusted network of specialist associates and partner organisations when needed. This approach allows us to stay lean and responsive, while bringing in the right expertise to meet the needs of each project.
