ABOUT US

Digital Inventions at a glance

At Digital Inventions, we believe that the right data, used the right way, can transform how non-profit organisations learn, grow, and deliver impact.

Founded by David Jacobs, who serves as Lead Consultant, Digital Inventions offers deep expertise grounded in two decades of experience in ICT, monitoring and evaluation (M&E), and non-profit leadership. Every project is personally led by David, ensuring a hands-on, thoughtful approach to solving complex data challenges.

Digital Inventions works flexibly, drawing on a trusted network of specialist associates and partner organisations when needed. This approach allows us to stay lean and responsive, while bringing in the right expertise to meet the needs of each project.

OUR APPROACH

How we can help you

Data is often collected primarily for funder reporting. We help organizations turn it into action so they can improve service to beneficiaries, build trust, ensure staff accountability, and drive learning.

We do this through Impact Nest, a practical, no-jargon methodology for clarifying what to measure and how. For organizations ready to go further, our Action Weaver platform (built on Microsoft Azure) enables rapid deployment of custom data systems that work in real time.

OUR SERVICES

How we do this

We turn data into a tool for action, not just compliance. Many non-profits collect data to satisfy funders, but struggle to use it for learning, improvement or decision-making. We help you close that gap by designing systems and strategies that are grounded, efficient and aligned with your mission.

Our services range from clarifying what success looks like to building real-time data systems that track progress and flag issues early. Whether you need to refine your Theory of Change, build an indicator matrix, design a data collection plan, or implement a smart dashboard, we bring a practical, user-focused approach to every step.

Refining your Theory of Change

We guide teams in clarifying how their work leads to impact. This involves mapping day-to-day activities to long-term social change goals through a well-articulated Theory of Change.

Building Indicator Matrices

We help identify the most relevant data points, select appropriate collection methods, and develop tools that ensure data is reliable, consistent and useful.

Designing Data Collection Strategies

We help identify the right data points to collect, choose suitable collection methods, and develop user-friendly tools. Throughout the process, we prioritise consistency, data quality and practical use.
